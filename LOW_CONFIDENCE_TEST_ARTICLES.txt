TEST A: AMBIGUOUS - Partially True Science Claim (Expected: 60-75% confidence)
================================================================================

Scientists at MIT have developed a new battery technology that could revolutionize 
electric vehicles. The lithium-metal batteries show promising results in laboratory 
tests, with energy density improvements of up to 50%. However, commercial viability 
remains uncertain as the technology faces challenges with durability and cost. 
Researchers estimate it may take 5-10 years before mass production is feasible.

Expected Results:
- ML: FAKE 60-80% (cautious - mentions "promising" but also "uncertain")
- DL: FAKE 70-85% (detects mixed signals)
- LLM: REAL 65-75% (understands nuanced scientific reporting)
- Swarm: LIKELY_REAL 60-70% (some credible sources may have similar news)

Why Low Confidence:
- Mix of positive and cautious language
- Real science but speculative timeline
- Not sensational but not fully verified
- Borderline between breakthrough and hype

- Obvious REAL (UN climate): 80-95% confidence

This proves system is NOT hardcoded!

================================================================================
                            WHAT TO OBSERVE
================================================================================

1. Confidence Variation:
   - High confidence (90-100%): Obvious fake/real
   - Medium confidence (60-80%): Borderline cases
   - Low confidence (40-60%): Ambiguous/unverifiable

2. Model Disagreement:
   - ML vs DL vs LLM will show different results
   - Proves each model has different strengths

3. Verdict Variety:
   - FAKE, LIKELY_FAKE, INCONCLUSIVE, LIKELY_REAL, REAL
   - Not just binary FAKE/REAL

4. Reasoning Quality:
   - LLM should explain WHY confidence is low
   - Agent Swarm should show lack of evidence

================================================================================
                            SUCCESS CRITERIA
================================================================================

System is working correctly if:
✓ Confidence varies (not always 100%)
✓ Models disagree on borderline cases
✓ INCONCLUSIVE verdicts appear
✓ Reasoning explains uncertainty
✓ Agent Swarm shows mixed/no evidence

System is hardcoded if:
✗ Always 100% confidence
✗ All models always agree
✗ Never shows INCONCLUSIVE
✗ Same result for different articles
